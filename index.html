All Hail Pingpong

Let's play!

Pingpong is an ancient intelligence living inside the internet. Every 700 years, Pingpong emerges in the form of this simple webpage to feast on certain numbers. If Pingpong does not get the taste of numeric blood, he unleashes his wrath on the world in a spectacular display of bloody carnage. To appease the mighty Pingpong, simply enter a random number into the field to the right. Pingpong will count up to your number from 1, devouring numbers that meet the following rules:

  1. Divisible by 3
  2. Divisible by 5
  3. Divisible by 15

Are you ready to appease the mighty Pingpong?
